Welcome to Bright RP — a serious and immersive DarkRP experience set in Downtown. Please read all rules carefully before playing. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. By connecting to our server you agree to abide by all rules listed here. Have fun and respect everyone!

General Rules
01
No RDM (Random Deathmatch) — Do not kill players without a valid in-character reason. All kills must be justified by roleplay. Ban
02
No Propkill / Prop Surfing / Prop Push — Using props to harm, move, or gain unfair advantages is strictly prohibited. Ban
03
No Hate Speech or Harassment — Racism, sexism, homophobia or targeted harassment of any player will result in an immediate permanent ban. Perm Ban
04
No Cheating or Exploiting — Hacks, scripts, map exploits, or bug abuse of any kind is permanently bannable. Perm Ban
05
No Spam — Do not spam chat, voice, adverts or admin calls. Kick
06
Respect all players and staff — Toxicity, trolling, and disrespecting admins will not be tolerated. Warn
07
No OOC toxicity — Keep out-of-character chat civil. No arguing, flaming or drama in OOC. Warn
RP
Roleplay Rules
01
NLR (New Life Rule) — When you die you forget everything from your past life. Do not return to your death location for 3 minutes.
02
FearRP — If a gun is pointed at you at close range and you are unarmed, you must comply.
03
No Metagaming — Do not use out-of-character information in-character (e.g. using player names above heads to target players).
04
Stay in character — Maintain your RP role at all times unless an admin asks you to go OOC.
05
No FailRP — Do not act in a way that makes no sense for your character or the roleplay scenario.
B
Building Rules
01
Building sign required — Place a sign when building. You may not be raided while it is up and must remove it when done.
02
No sky bases — Bases must be built on the ground in owned properties only.
03
No one-way props — Shooting through materials you cannot see through is not allowed.
04
Fading doors — Maximum 2 fading doors per base entrance. Must be openable by keypad or button.
05
No blocking public areas — Do not block roads, spawn, PD, or any public building with props.
$$
Raiding Rules
01
Raid cooldown — You may not raid the same base more than once every 20 minutes.
02
Raid timer — Raids must end within 10 minutes of starting.
03
No raiding while building — Respect the building sign. Do not raid bases displaying one.
04
Advert required — You must /advert Raid before beginning. No advert = RDM.
05
No camping — Do not camp inside or outside a base after a raid has ended.
CP
Civil Protection Rules
01
No corrupt CP — Civil Protection must uphold the law at all times and cannot be bribed.
02
Warrant required — You must have a valid reason and warrant to raid/search a property.
03
No abusing arrest — Do not arrest players without a valid reason. RDA is punishable.
04
No PD camping — Civil Protection must actively patrol and RP, not sit in spawn.
